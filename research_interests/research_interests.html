Research Interests

What are my professional interests and experiences?

Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCIs)

My interests lie in the development of real-time Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCIs). BCIs record the electrical activity of your brain and map them into control signals to help individuals with paralysis control external devices. BCIs are complex engineering systems that have the potential to help these individuals restore their motor function and ability to communicate. Proven applications include controlling computer cursors , controlling robotic limbs , and restoring speech
